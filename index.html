📖 Our Story

Namma Kalappai was born from a simple belief — that the best food comes from nature, processed only by honest hands. We started as a small family initiative in Tamil Nadu, sourcing raw honey directly from forest beekeepers and cold-pressed oils from traditional chekku mills.

The name "Kalappai" (கலப்பை) means plough — the symbol of farming, hard work, and connection to the earth. We carry that spirit in every product we deliver.

Today we serve over 5000+ families across Tamil Nadu, offering everything from forest honey and multi-millet health mixes to A2 ghee and cold-pressed oils — all made the traditional, honest way.
